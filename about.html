Welcome to Our World

We are Dave and Georgina Roberts, living near the outskirts of Snowdonia National Park in North West Wales. By day, Georgina and Dave work in the local hospital. Outside of work, we enjoy walking and cycling whenever we can.

Our shared passion for travel, Celtic heritage, and exploring new places inspired us to start this blog.

From scenic countryside walks to exotic adventures abroad, we hope our experiences spark your curiosity and encourage you to pack your bags for your own unforgettable journey.

Our Story

A Chance Encounter

We first met in 2007 in a café, enjoyed a coffee, and then went our separate ways. The following day, while I was driving to visit friends in Gloucester, I overtook a Renault Kangoo that looked strikingly familiar. When I glanced over, I realised it was Georgina. I motioned for her to pull into the next lay-by—and that was the start of our journey together.

Since then our lives have revolved around exploring the great outdoors—cycling, walking, and travelling in our beloved Volkswagen campervan. We’ve journeyed across Europe and visited many of Scotland’s islands, including the Outer Hebrides, Orkney, and Shetland.

Happily sharing our adventures since 2007

Celebrating Celtic Heritage

Proudly Welsh & Cornish

Dave’s Welsh background and Georgina’s Cornish roots create a unique blend of Celtic traditions. We find joy in discovering historical landmarks, connecting with local cultures, and indulging in local delicacies—like a hearty Welsh rarebit or a traditional Cornish pasty.

Every year, we set out to uncover new routes, ports, boat events, car shows, and festivals, all while soaking up the wonderful sights, sounds, and tastes along the way.

Sharing Celtic pride wherever we go

Join Us on This Journey

We invite you to explore our blog and follow our adventures around the UK and Europe. From rugged coastlines to hidden countryside paths, each trip is a chance to discover something new about the world—and ourselves.
