About Us

Our Story

We are Dave and Georgina Roberts who live near the outskirts of Snowdonia National Park in North West Wales. I was raised in the royal town of Caernarfon in Gwynedd, and Georgina is half Cornish and half Devonshire – her father's family were from St Ives in Cornwall and her mother's family were from Plymouth.

We first met in 2007 in a café. We had a coffee and got to know each other, then departed and got into our cars and drove away. The next day, I was driving to visit friends in Gloucester. I was on the A5 in Shropshire when I overtook a Renault Kangoo that looked similar to the one I saw in the café. I looked over and saw that the driver was Georgina. We both exchanged a look, and I pointed at her, gesturing, “Go and park in the next lay-by.” When I stopped, the first thing I said to her was, “Oh my God, I can't believe this.”

From that moment, the rest is history! Since then, our lifestyle has been geared around walking and cycling in the local countryside and travelling in our beloved Volkswagen campervan to various places in Europe and the islands of Scotland, such as the Outer Hebrides, Orkney, and Shetland.

We hope you enjoy following our journeys around the UK and Europe.
