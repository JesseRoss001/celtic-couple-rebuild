About Us

Our Story

We are Dave and Georgina Roberts who live near the outskirts of Snowdonia National Park in North West Wales. Dave was raised in Caernarfon in Gwynedd, and Georgina is half Cornish and half Devonshire.

Our lifestyle revolves around walking, cycling and road-tripping in our VW campervan across Europe and the Scottish isles.
