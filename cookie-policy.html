Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Celtic Couple uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly (essential cookies).
  • Gather anonymous statistics about site usage (analytics cookies).
  • Remember your cookie-consent choices.

3. Types of Cookies We Set

CookiePurposeExpiry
cc_cookie_consentSaves your cookie preferences.6 months
_cc_analyticsAggregated page-view counts (no personal data).24 hours

4. Third-Party Cookies

We keep third-party tracking to an absolute minimum. Embedded social-media content (e.g. YouTube, Instagram) may set their own cookies; these are outside our control and governed by the respective platform’s policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings. Doing so will not break this site, but certain preference-based features might not remember your choices between visits.

6. Consent Banner

On your first visit you will see a small banner asking you to accept or decline analytics cookies. Essential cookies are loaded regardless, as they are required for the website to function.

7. Updates

We may revise this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will appear on this page with an updated date below.
